About Text Bridge

Text Bridge is a service that allows you to stay connected to your Facebook contacts without the need of WiFi, data connection, or even their phone number. That being said, Textbridge is not meant to replace SMS, but rather act as a complement to ensure that everyone remains connected to their network despite their accessibility to the internet. Simply have a cell signal, and instantly message anyone you know on Facebook!
